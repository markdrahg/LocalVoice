About LocalVoice

LocalVoice is a platform created for Ghanaians to raise awareness about the challenges in their communities. Users can upload images and videos that showcase issues like poor roads, sanitation problems, lack of water, or electricity.

Our Goal

Our goal is to give the public a voice encouraging government agencies and well-meaning individuals to take action and bring about real change in local areas.

Our Mission

To amplify the voices of everyday citizens and bring community issues to light.

Our Vision

A Ghana where no issue goes unheard and every voice matters.
