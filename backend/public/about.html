About LocalVoice

LocalVoice is a grassroots platform dedicated to amplifying the authentic voices of Ghana’s underserved communities. Through user-generated photos, videos, and narratives, we bring hidden challenges into the public eye, fostering transparency and driving tangible action.

Our Goal

To create an accessible hub where every citizen can share their community’s struggles, establishing a living repository of issues for NGOs, government agencies, and individuals to discover and address.

Our Mission

To empower local storytellers with a reliable platform that connects grassroots experiences to resources and decision-makers, driving sustainable improvements across sectors like infrastructure, education, health, and sanitation.

Our Vision

A Ghana where informed communities collaborate seamlessly with institutions and allies, ensuring every voice is heard and every challenge is addressed.

Key Values

  • Transparency: Open sharing of information and responses.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming all communities and contributors.
  • Accountability: Public tracking of interventions and outcomes.
  • Collaboration: Bridging citizens, NGOs, and government.
